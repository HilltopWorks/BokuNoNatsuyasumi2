&1
-Chinese Peacock-
These large butterflies
suddenly pop out when
I step into the woods.
Their inky black wings
shine so pretty.

&2
-Long Tail Spangle-
Listening to the gentle
bubbling of the brook, I
look up to find them
frolicking. Their elegant
dance in flight etches
a memory on my mind.

&3
-Chinese Windmill-
They effortlessly glide
through the air with
minimal flapping. Their
wing patterns remind
me of my aunt's
clothes.
